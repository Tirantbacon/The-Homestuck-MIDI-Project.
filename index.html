Q. - Can I use your MIDIs/music?
R. - Yes, as long as you give credit to me.

Q. - Which software are you using to make music?
R. - FL Studio. Sometimes FamiTracker.

Q. - Which soundfonts/synthesizers do you use?
R. - FluidR3 and TimGM from MuseScore, some original
sounds from SNES and FL Studio Harmless VST.

Q. - Make this MIDI, pleeeeeeease!!!!!
R. - No. I'll make all of them... eventually. Just wait.

Q. - Can I help you... somehow?
R. - You can donate some GOLD to me so I could keep this page.
However, maybe you need it to pay college.

Q. - Where is the DONATE button?
R. - Below.

Q. - Can I use your pixel-art as avatar or whatever I could use them?
R. - Same as 1st question.

Q. - Which software do you use to draw covers?
R. - MS Paint and, sometimes, Gimp.
